Navigating Policy Engagement

A Practitioner's Guidebook

Review Draft — This guidebook is under development. Chapters are being edited collaboratively and shared here for review. Last updated: 7 February 2026.

This guidebook helps conservation practitioners engage effectively with policy processes. It provides practical tools for assessing the political landscape, planning strategic engagement, and measuring progress—grounded in the realities of how policy actually works.

  • Chapter 1
    Understanding Policy Processes Complete

    The foundations: how policy works, policy windows, the attribution challenge, and why evidence alone doesn't change minds.

  • Chapter 2
    Assessing the Policy Context Complete

    Tools for understanding your context: team readiness, the Policy Context Tool, stakeholder analysis, and supporting assessment methods.

  • Chapter 3
    Planning Policy Engagement Coming Soon

    Defining goals, selecting strategies, building a theory of change, and identifying enablers and barriers.

  • Chapter 4
    Measuring Success Coming Soon

    Approaches for measuring influence: outcome harvesting, contribution analysis, and the RPSB framework.
